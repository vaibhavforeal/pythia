Pythia is a free web app for Vedic astrology and numerology. Enter your date, time and place of birth and Pythia computes an accurate sidereal (Lahiri) birth chart with the Swiss Ephemeris — then lets you explore it in plain language with an AI guide: your rashi and nakshatra, dasha timing, yogas, marriage compatibility (Guna Milan), doshas and remedies.

Your account & sign-in

Pythia needs a free account so it can privately save the charts and conversations you create, and keep them tied to you across visits. You can sign up with an email address and password, or use Sign in with Google.

When you choose Sign in with Google, Pythia uses only your name, email address and basic profile to create and secure your account. It never posts on your behalf, reads your Gmail, or accesses any other Google data. Exactly what we store and why is described in our Privacy Policy; your use of Pythia is covered by our Terms of Service.
